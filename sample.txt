University Admission Guide - Fall 2026

Admission Fees:
The application fee for undergraduate programs is $75, non-refundable.
The application fee for graduate programs is $100, non-refundable.
Fee waivers are available for students demonstrating financial hardship.

Application Deadlines:
Early Decision applications must be submitted by November 1.
Regular Decision applications must be submitted by January 15.
Transfer student applications must be submitted by March 1.

Required Documents:
1. Completed online application form
2. Official high school or college transcripts
3. Two letters of recommendation
4. Personal statement (500-650 words)
5. Standardized test scores (optional for 2026 admissions)

Scholarships:
The Merit Scholarship covers up to 50% of tuition for students with a GPA above 3.8.
The Need-Based Grant is awarded based on the FAFSA application.
International students may apply for the Global Excellence Scholarship.

Campus Housing:
On-campus housing is guaranteed for first-year students.
Housing applications open on May 1 and close on July 15.
Average dormitory cost is $8,500 per academic year.
